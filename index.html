About Us

Here at SynthBrew™, we are the leading pioneers in interstellar coffee service. Out here, in the outer reaches of the Solar System, access to ingredients is scarce and so we have devised an industry leading process to create coffee from the inorganic material all around us. We recognise that the competition for coffee this far out is non-existent and so we admit our products aren't very good, however, we also recognise you have no choice if you want your caffeine fix. This is why we have chosen not to have competitive pricing and will absolutely drain you of your funds where possible. We are proud to offer an extremely limited variety of coffee and pastry products that are made with no love nor care as we just get the robot to do the job for us.


Why would you drink here? Honestly, we ask ourselves the same question everyday which, admittedly, isn't very often considering a day here is roughly 30 years on Earth. Did we mention we pay our workers a daily salary? As far as we know that's better than all the Earth coffee shops who pay monthly or weekly. But we just told you a day here is 30 years on Earth, should this worry you? Absolutely! Do we care? No.
